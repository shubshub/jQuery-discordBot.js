The rules of Text Ninja is Simple

Each player Selects a Ninjitsu Attack Style

- High Attack
- Mid Attack
- Low Attack

Similar to Rock Paper Scissors
Except that to win you must meet one of these conditions

Get 3 Wins 1 for each type of attack
Get 3 Wins in 1 Attack Type

High Attack Jumps over a Low Attack and Dodges it Beating Low Attack
Mid Attack intercepts the High Attack foiling the High Attack and beating it
Low Attack Trips the Mid Attack and Beats it

Be sharp though as the AI will Adapt based on the data it has access to
Either player may look at the Scoreboard of the game at any point
